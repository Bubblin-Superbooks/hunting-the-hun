Chapter XIII

Tips For Recruits

The four best friends of the fighting man are: (1 His rifle; (2 the entrenching tool; (3) the oil sheet; (4) small box respirator (gas mask).) )

The best thing to do with your rifle is to get an old sock, cut about eight inches from the top part and pull it over the rifle muzzle on to the breech. That will prevent the wet clay from interfering with the working of the bolt. Your entrenching tool is invaluable, for when you have gained a position at night your first duty
